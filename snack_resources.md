## Goal
Gradually replace processed snacks with healthier homemade alternatives.

## Healthy Snack Ideas by Category

### Fruit
- Ripe pear or apple slices
- Grapes (seedless)
- Mango cubes
- Strawberries

### Dairy
- Yogurt pouch or small yogurt cup
- Cheese cubes or sticks
- Cottage cheese

### Protein
- Hard-boiled egg
- Tuna onigiri / rice ball
- Homemade chicken nuggets (baked)

### Crunchy
- Rice cakes
- Whole grain crackers
- Plain popcorn

### Vegetables
- Edamame
- Cauliflower bites (baked in butter)
- Corn on the cob (mini)
- Cherry tomatoes

## Online Inspiration
- Search "healthy school snack ideas" + your country for local options
- Look for snack guides from your national health authority
